## Dark Energy and Dark Matter

A foundational framework using astrophysics metaphors for architectural forces.

> "Dark energy is an anti-gravity that is forcing matter apart and accelerating the expansion of the universe. Dark matter is an invisible matter that has a gravitational effect on stars and galaxies."

### Dark Energy Forces (driving decomposition)
- Simple components
- Team autonomy
- Fast deployment pipeline
- Support multiple technology stacks
- Segregate by characteristics

### Dark Matter Forces (resisting decomposition)
- Simple interactions
- Efficient interactions
- Prefer ACID over BASE
- Minimize runtime coupling
- Minimize design-time coupling
